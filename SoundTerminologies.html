SOUND TERMINOLOGIES

ECHO

Reflection of sound that returns after some time delay.

Example. Sound bouncing off a mountain

REVERBERATION

Persistence of sound due to multiple reflections in a closed space.

Example. Sound in a concert hall

INTERFERENCE

Interaction between two or more sound waves that can amplify or cancel each other.

Example. Beats in music

RESONANCE

When an object vibrates at its natural frequency due to another vibrating source.

Example. Breaking glass with loud sound
